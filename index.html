About

I am a Ph.D. candidate in Computer Science at Old Dominion University, working with Prof. Lusi Li on trustworthy machine learning. My research centers on energy-based models and their use for robust representation learning, with applications to multi-view clustering, semantic communication, and AI security — including the robustness of vision-language pre-trained models under adversarial perturbation.

I'm broadly interested in building learning systems that stay reliable when data is incomplete, noisy, or adversarial, and in understanding what that means for AI deployed in the real world.
